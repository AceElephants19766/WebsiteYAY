Meet Us

Who Are We?

We are Ace Elephants #19766, a determined young robotics team from Modi'in-Maccabim-Reut participating in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC).

Our team consists of students passionate about technology, creation, and innovation. We don't just build robots – we learn to work as a team, solve complex problems, and give back to our community. Our goal is to break boundaries, learn, and most importantly – enjoy the journey!
